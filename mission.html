Mission

In the next couple of weeks, I will regularly update this post as I embark on a mission of defining the following:

    Thinking Frameworks
      Computational Thinking
      Inferential Thinking
      Algorithmic Thinking
      Critical Thinking
    Core Concepts in the following areas (and more, perhaps including physical and social sciences):
      Machine Learning
      Computer Networking
      Database System
      Compiler

I'm trying to concretely define the above thinking frameworks, as succintly as possible. They would form the basis of my thought processes, which I could invoke in times of need.

Besides that, I would like to capture the core concepts in each field as concisely as possible. I seek to enumerate the most fundamental concepts from which everything is built upon. I have started to realize that it is impossible to remember every single detail about every area at any given time. However, I hope that, by comprehending the core concepts, I will be able to make connections between previously unrelated concepts, which is the very definition of innovation.
